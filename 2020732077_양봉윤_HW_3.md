# P3.1  
$\text{상태변수 설정}$  
$x_1(t) = y(t) \ , \ x_2(t) = \frac{d y(t)}{dt}$  

$[운동방정식]$  
$M \cdot \frac{d^2 y(t)}{dt^2} = F(t) - b \cdot \frac{d y(t)}{dt} - k y(t)$  

$[상태변수로 1차미분방정식 표현]$  
$M \cdot \dot{x_2}(t))  = F(t) - b x_2(t) - k x_1(t)$   
$\dot{x_2}(t) = \frac{-k}{M} \dot{x_1}(t) - \frac{b}{M} x_2(t) + \frac{1}{M} F(t)$
